Lychee Matcha Latte with Lychee Cream Cheese Foam

Sweet lychee meets earthy matcha under a cloud of cream cheese foam. One of those drinks that feels a little extra in the best way.

Ingredients

  • 3g matcha powder
  • 30g hot water
  • 10g maple syrup
  • 100ml milk

Method

  1. Sift matcha
  2. Whisk
  3. Add milk
