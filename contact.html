Contact

Hi, I am Amy. I am based in Toronto and I run How About Saturday as a one-person creative project alongside my work as a Registered Behaviour Analyst. Whether you want to talk about a recipe, a brand collaboration, or just say hello, I would love to hear from you.

✉️

Email

For brand partnerships, collaborations, UGC work, or general enquiries, email is the best way to reach me. I aim to respond within 48 hours.

Instagram

I am most active on Instagram where I share new recipes, behind the scenes, and the occasional matcha. DMs are open for quick questions.

Work with me

I work with food, beverage, and lifestyle brands on a range of projects including sponsored recipe development, UGC content, Instagram reels, and long-term partnerships. My audience is engaged, niche, and genuinely interested in tea, matcha, and thoughtful home baking.

Current brand partners and collaborations include DAVIDsTEA, The Roasters Pack, Flavorish, and Emeri. If you would like to discuss working together, please reach out via email with a brief overview of your brand and what you have in mind.
